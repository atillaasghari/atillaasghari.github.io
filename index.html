    About Me

    Hi I’m Atilla, I am a computer engineering student at Azad University of Urmia. Since childhood, I have had a strong interest in computers and technology. Initially, my focus was more on hardware and electronics. I used to assemble and disassemble my computer and create electronic circuits, often building new things with electronic components from my toys. In 1395 (2016/2017), at the age of 15, I obtained a certificate as a general computer hardware repair technician.

    Later, I became familiar with the Python programming language, marking the beginning of my journey into the programming world in 1398 (2019/2020). After Python, I started learning various programming languages. I was eager to learn everything, but I realized that I needed to narrow down the languages for my goals. I identified Python, C/C++, PHP, and a small amount of JavaScript as the languages I could use for my purposes and continued learning them. Alongside programming, I developed a keen interest in writing articles. Because of this, I learned HTML/CSS to create my personal website. This allows me to share my projects and articles on the platform.
